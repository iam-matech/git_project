NIGERIAN JOLLOF RECIPES!

Hey my dear fellow Nigerians, let us all gather here and prepare our tasty Jollof.

Below are the main ingredients we need

  • Rice
  • Pepper
  • Tomato paste
  • Groundnut oil
  • Seasoning
  • Curry and Thyme
  • Proteins

Having gathered our ingredients, it is time to out them together to make our tasty Jollof.
  1. Wash a pot and parboil your preffered quantity of rice
  2. Take out the parboiled rice and leave in a seive to dry out
  3. You then to making your paste for the jollof
  4. Add in dry pan a bit of groundnut oil, leave to heat for some seconds
  5. Pour in your pepper mixed with tomato paste, fry these for a bit then add your seasoning
  6. Having fried your pepper, pour a bit of water then add your rice
  7. Cook till rice is soft enough while turning the rice from time to time, making sure everything blends in well
  8. Having achieved your preffered rice texture and taste, turn off the fire

    9. Voila, your tasty jollof is ready to be served!
